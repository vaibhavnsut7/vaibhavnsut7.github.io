Whether you want to take center stage or work behind the scenes, a degree in media and communication can open up many media careers. Media and communication professionals convey meaningful, captivating, or insightful information to their audiences, helping keep the public informed. Careers in communication are diverse, and talented communication professionals are in demand. Most pathways in the field require a bachelor's degree in communication or a related area.
